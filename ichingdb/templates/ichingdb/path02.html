Example Path 2

Below is an example of a path through the I Ching's space of meaning, which can be interpreted as an outline of a story.


䷌ 13 Harmonizing People

䷘ 25 Disentangling

Reading

Retreat and prepare a countermove. This may seem like a disaster but if you move with it you can eliminate the old negativity. This is not the time for aggression or insistence on your ideas. Accumulate the strength to carry heavy loads and confront difficult situations. Proceed step by step. Gather energy for a decisive new move.

䷘ 25 Disentangling

䷉ 10 Treading

Reading

If you realize what this time means, all your plans will succeed. Smooth the way from the shadows. You have a hidden connection that binds you to others. Empty your mind of immediate goals. Supportive friends will soon arrive. Step out to meet the new destiny. You can confront and disable a powerful and malevolent opponent. Gather energy for a decisive new move.

䷉ 10 Treading

䷥ 38 Diverging

Reading

You must decisively separate yourself from a dangerous influence. Have no fear. Face the danger. Have no concern for glory or achievements. Locate yourself in the center and nourish your people. All cause for discord and sorrow will disappear. Step outside the norms and re-imagine the situation. Co-operate with the ongoing process of change.

䷥ 38 Diverging

䷔ 21 Biting Through

Reading

In an unexpected way you meet someone or something of great importance. Let this influence enter your heart. Though you may feel your gifts are small, bite through the obstacles to union. Act from your heart. You can create a warm and inspiring atmosphere. Care for other people. Gather energy for a decisive new move.

䷔ 21 Biting Through

䷝ 30 Radiance / Visionary

Reading

Biting through to union you encounter something old and poisonous. Bring it out. Beat your drums or you will end up lamenting like a very old person. The distress you feel will soon disappear. Those who approach you now are not outlaws. Treat them with great respect and seek an alliance. Do not be afraid to act alone. You are connected with a creative force.

䷝ 30 Radiance / Visionary

䷍ 14 Great Being

Reading

You have found the connection. Light and power surge up from below. As you venture into danger you will get what you need by being flexible and adaptable. Gather people around you and do not impose your will. You are at the center of this group. Do not let this chance slip through your fingers. Use an inspiring idea to carry your desires. Be resolute. You are connected with a creative force.


In this example each hexagram has only a single changing line. And although there is a complex reading for each transition only the text for the changing line is shown to give an idea of the nature of the transition.

This is just one of many thousands of possible paths through this space of meaning. It serves as an example of how the hexagrams connect via changing lines to form a complex network of meaning. This is an alternate way to get to know the wisdom encoded within the I Ching. Each path can also be elaborated into stories to illustrate the wisdom and provide guidance to help people navigate their way through life.
