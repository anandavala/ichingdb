Just click in the query field, type your query text then hit 'ENTER' or click 'Submit'.

The lines and changing lines will then be randomised using the "3 coin method" (with the 'seed' set using the current time).

All consultations are recorded in the archive, however there is no identifying information stored along with them.

This growing archive is an anonymous indicator of the prevailing questions on people's minds and the corresponding responses from the I Ching.

Note: do not think of this like querying a search engine, think of it as like asking a wise person or even an omniscient universal consciousness.

So if you ask a flippant or meaningless question it will not just respond to the question, but to the person who asked it.
