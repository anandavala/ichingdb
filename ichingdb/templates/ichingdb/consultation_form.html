Just click in the query field, type your query text then hit 'ENTER' or click 'Submit'.

The lines and changing lines will then be randomised using the "3 coin method" (with the 'seed' set using the current time).

All generated consultations are recorded in the archive and can be referred back to later, however there is no identifying information stored along with them.

Alternatively, to look up a reading without generating it randomly, e.g. 12 → 1 just append "12/1/" to the main URL. Or if there are no changing lines in the reading enter the initial hexagram twice, e.g. "12/12/"

Note: do not think of this like querying a search engine, think of it as like asking a wise person or even an omniscient universal consciousness.

If you sincerely seek to live life in accord with the Way and align with the universal flow, then the I Ching will speak clearly to give you guidance and insight.
