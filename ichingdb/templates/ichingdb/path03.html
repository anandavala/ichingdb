䷲ 51 Shake / Groundbreaker

䷏ 16 Providing for

Reading

This is a profound shock. Everything is turned upside down. Do not call out for help now. Cut through pride and complication and set the armies marching. If you meet people with generosity and care you can signal to your true kin. Step outside the norms. These goings and comings will release the energy bound up in the past. Gather energy for a decisive new move.

䷏ 16 Providing for

䷢ 35 Flourishing

Reading

Work on in the dark to provide for the future. Keep your pride out of the way. Your inner work will call out to others. Master your words and you can influence people. Attack the old ways and you will soon emerge into the light. These goings and comings release energy bound up in the past. Step outside the norms. Gather energy for the decisive new move.

䷢ 35 Flourishing

䷿ 64 Not Yet Crossing

Reading

You feel anxious and sorrowful. Do not fear. Take on this arduous task and receive the blessing of the feminine powers. Let yourself be lured into action. Your sincerity and dedication are what count. The situation seems difficult, but you will survive. This will bring profit and insight in the end. Gather energy for a decisive new move.

䷿ 64 Not Yet Crossing

䷧ 40 Deliverance / Liberating

Reading

You have a great connection but start slowly. Let yourself be lured on. Difficulties are going, praise and ease are coming. Pull back and join with others and you will capture the force opposing your plans and desires. There is nothing that will not benefit. These goings and comings release the energy now bound up in the past. Cooperate with the on-going process of change.

䷧ 40 Deliverance / Liberating

䷵ 54 Marrying Maiden

Reading

Act on your plans. You are in the right position. Difficulties are going, ripeness and abundance are coming. Talk to those who can connect you with what is great. Now is the time to put things in order. If you let yourself be led you can realize your hidden potential. Co-operate with the ongoing process of change.

䷵ 54 Marrying Maiden

䷡ 34 Great Invigorating

Reading

You are in a subservient place. Have patience. In the end you will reverse things and gain the strategic position. You are confronting a troublesome past experience. Retreat to rekindle your love for people. This is a resting-place and a portent of future happiness. If you use force now you will only be entangled. You are coupled with a creative force.


In this example each hexagram has only a single changing line. And although there is a complex reading for each transition only the text for the changing line is shown to give an idea of the nature of the transition.

This is just one of many thousands of possible paths through this space of meaning. It serves as an example of how the hexagrams connect via changing lines to form a complex network of meaning. This is an alternate way to get to know the wisdom encoded within the I Ching. Each path can also be elaborated into stories to illustrate the wisdom and provide guidance to help people navigate their way through life.
